About Me

Education Researcher & Innovative Educator

With a PhD in Educational Psychology from Stanford University, I specialize in examining how emerging technologies can enhance learning outcomes in higher education settings. My work focuses on creating accessible, engaging educational experiences that bridge traditional pedagogy with digital innovations.

I currently serve as Associate Professor in the Department of Education at New Horizon University, where I lead several initiatives to redesign curriculum frameworks for the digital age.
